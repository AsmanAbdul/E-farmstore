WELCOME

Who we are

  • e–granary is a mobile-based digital platform that facilitates the meeting of demand and supply of agricultural products and inputs, reduces the cost to serve and risks to enable service providers to gainfully invest/serve smallholder farmers, and offers targeted agronomical support throughout the growing season giving smallholder farmers support from seed to market for increased productivity, incomes and livelihoods.
What we do?

– our business model hinges on delivering the maximum value to farmers in maize, common and soya beans value chains, typically small holding farmers, in a transparent and convenient manner. e—granary procures directly from farmers, producer coops, and community-based organizations and manages the value chain from farm gate to a buyer’s doorstep. this allows farmers to focus on production while leveraging their collective strength to sell their produce.

How we do it

1

Crop Management And Support

– knowledge delivery through mobile and web platforms

2

Credit And Insurance

– access to credit helps farmers gain access to agri-inputs, farm equipment, and post-harvest services

3

Group Purchase Of Agricultural Inputs

– group purchase reduces cost for farmers and increases sales through the platform

4

Coordination Of Post Harvest Services

– providing access to post-harvest services providers to ensure farmers capture the maximum crop value

5

Coordination Of Contract Farming

– making farm equipment affordable with micro-finance support

We've been able to attract key partners along, creating an ecosystem that is beneficial to smallholder farmers.

Zero Hunger

– one-third of the world's 7.3 billion people are smallholders farmers and their families who produce nearly 70 percent of all food consumed worldwide on 60 percent of the planet's arable land. e–granary is contributing to the agenda of feeding 9 billion people by 2050 through helping smallholder farmers access certified inputs affordable credit and a reliable market for better production and improved livelihoods. Gender Equality
